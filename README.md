# ICICI-Prudential-Gold-ETF-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI
End-to-end Power BI project analyzing Gold ETF data to deliver insights on trends, volume, and market performance.

# 📊 ICICI Prudential Gold ETF Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the performance of the ICICI Prudential Gold ETF using Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw financial data into meaningful insights to support better decision-making.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Financial datasets are complex and difficult to interpret quickly. Investors and analysts face challenges in:
- Tracking price trends
- Comparing performance over time
- Identifying high trading volumes
- Understanding 52-week high and low patterns

---

## 💡 Solution
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that simplifies financial data and provides clear insights through visualization.

---

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Collection
- Collected historical ETF data from NSE
- Data includes:
  - Open, Close, High, Low
  - Volume, VWAP
  - 52 Week High & Low
  - Date (Day, Month, Year)

---

### 2️⃣ Data Cleaning
- Removed null and duplicate values
- Standardized data formats
- Converted date column into structured format

---

### 3️⃣ Data Transformation (DAX)
- Created measures:
  - Total Volume
  - Average 52W High & Low
  - Price trends
- Performed time-based analysis:
  - Daily
  - Monthly
  - Quarterly

---

### 4️⃣ Data Modeling
- Built relationships between data fields
- Created hierarchies:
  - Year → Quarter → Month → Day

---

### 5️⃣ Dashboard Development
- Designed KPI Cards:
  - Avg 52W High & Low
  - Total Volume
  - Open & Close Price

- Created Visualizations:
  - Quarterly comparison
  - Daily trends
  - Monthly volume analysis
  - Price movement analysis

- Added Filters:
  - Year
  - Quarter
  - Month
  - Day

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Highest trading volume observed in January and February
- Price trends are relatively stable on weekdays
- Quarterly comparison shows performance variation
- Volume spikes indicate increased market activity

---

## 🎯 Business Impact
- Reduces analysis time
- Helps in faster decision-making
- Improves understanding of financial trends
- Useful for investors and analysts
